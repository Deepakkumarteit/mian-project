At Amrit Mantrah, we ensure that all our products are manufactured, packaged, and delivered with the highest quality and safety standards. Please read this Refund & Return Policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. No Refund on Product Effectiveness

  • Our products are Ayurvedic wellness supplements. Results may vary depending on lifestyle, diet, and medical condition.
  • As per industry standards and AYUSH/FSSAI guidelines, no refunds or returns will be provided on the grounds of product effectiveness or perceived lack of results.

2. Order Cancellation (Before Dispatch)

  • Orders can be cancelled within 6 hours of placing the order if they have not been shipped.
  • In such cases, a full refund will be processed to the original payment method within 3 business days.

3. Order Cancellation (In-Transit)

If a customer cancels an order after dispatch but before delivery, the product must first be returned to us.

  • Once the product is received back in our warehouse in unused and sealed condition, a refund will be issued.
  • Delivery/shipping charges will be deducted from the refund amount.

4. No Return/Refund After Delivery

Due to the nature of our products (health supplements), we do not accept returns or provide refunds once the product has been delivered and accepted by the customer. This policy is to ensure product safety, hygiene, and compliance with health standards.

5. Damaged / Wrong Product

  • If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photo/video proof.
  • After verification, we will arrange a replacement or issue a full refund (as applicable).

6. Refund Timelines

  • Eligible refunds will be processed within 3 business days after product inspection and approval.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method only.
